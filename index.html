Common Roofing Problems and Solutions

Roofs face a myriad of challenges over time, from weathering the elements to enduring physical damage. Recognizing these issues early and addressing them properly is essential for maintaining the integrity and longevity of your roof. Here’s a rundown of common roofing problems we encounter in Rochester Hills, along with how our services offer effective solutions:

Water Leaks

The Problem: Water leaks are among the most prevalent roofing issues, often stemming from damaged shingles, faulty flashing, or deteriorated underlayment. Unaddressed leaks can lead to significant structural damage and mold growth within your home.

How We Solve It: Our expert technicians conduct thorough inspections to pinpoint the source of any leaks. We then provide targeted repairs, replacing damaged materials and reinforcing vulnerable areas to ensure a watertight seal and prevent future water intrusion.

Missing or Damaged Shingles

The Problem: Wind, hail, and age can all contribute to shingles becoming loose, cracked, or completely missing. This exposes the underlayment to the elements, potentially leading to leaks and accelerated deterioration of the roof structure.

How We Solve It: We offer prompt shingle replacement services, using high-quality materials that match your existing roof in style and color. Our precise installation ensures a secure fit and restores your roof’s protective barrier against the elements.

Damaged Flashing

The Problem: Flashing, the metal strips installed around chimneys, vents, and other roof penetrations, can corrode, become dislodged, or otherwise fail over time. Damaged flashing allows water to seep into your home, causing leaks and water damage.

How We Solve It: We carefully inspect and repair or replace damaged flashing, ensuring a watertight seal around all roof penetrations. Our durable flashing solutions protect your roof from water intrusion and prolong its lifespan.

Clogged Gutters

The Problem: Gutters clogged with leaves, debris, and other obstructions prevent rainwater from draining properly. This can cause water to back up onto your roof, leading to leaks, rot, and ice dam formation during the winter months.

How We Solve It: We offer comprehensive gutter cleaning services to remove debris and ensure proper water flow. Additionally, we can install gutter guards to prevent future clogs and reduce the need for frequent cleaning.

Sagging Roof Deck

The Problem: A sagging roof deck indicates underlying structural issues, such as water damage, rot, or inadequate support. This can compromise the integrity of your entire roof and pose a safety hazard.

How We Solve It: Our experienced team assesses the extent of the damage and provides structural repairs to reinforce the roof deck. We address the underlying cause of the sagging and ensure that your roof is structurally sound and safe.

Granule Loss from Asphalt Shingles

The Problem: Over time, asphalt shingles naturally lose granules, which provide protection from UV rays. Excessive granule loss can accelerate the deterioration of the shingles and reduce their lifespan.

How We Solve It: While some granule loss is normal, we can assess the severity and recommend appropriate solutions. In cases of significant granule loss, shingle replacement may be necessary to maintain the roof’s protective qualities.

Ice Dams

The Problem: Ice dams form when snow melts on a warm roof and refreezes at the eaves, creating a dam that prevents water from draining properly. This can cause water to back up under the shingles, leading to leaks and interior damage.

How We Solve It: We help prevent ice dams by ensuring proper attic insulation and ventilation to maintain a consistent roof temperature. We can also remove existing ice dams safely and recommend solutions to prevent future formation.

Roof rot or decay

The Problem: Roof rot and decay comes from the problem of water coming in contact with materials over a long duration, usually going unnoticed before there becomes a bigger problem. This leads to the breakdown of material and compromise of the roof which therefore needs replacement of some kind.

How We Solve It: Rochester Hills Roof Pro assesses extent of damage and replace rotten, decaying, or compromised wood, ensuring structural stability. In addition, we address any underlying issues, and advise and act on proper insulation to maintain roof and proper health in the long-term.
